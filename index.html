Welcome to Our Internship Project

We are a team of five Computer Science Engineering students. This website is our first internship project where we showcase what we are learning during our cloud computing internship.

About Our Internship

During our internship, we are learning Cloud Computing, AWS, DevOps, Git, and GitHub. We are gaining hands-on experience with AWS services such as Amazon EC2, Amazon S3, and CloudFront, along with DevOps tools like Docker and Terraform. This internship is helping us build practical skills in cloud infrastructure, deployment, and modern development workflows.
